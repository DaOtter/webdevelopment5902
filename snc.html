PRESS RELEASE
SNC Signs Charter with Northrop Grumman and General Atomics Aeronautical Systems to Deliver Freedom Trainer™ for UJTS

SNC, a global leader in aerospace and national security, today announced it signed an Executive Charter with Northrop Grumman (NYSE: NOC) and General Atomics Aeronautical Systems, Inc. (GA-ASI) to define and execute production and manufacturing teaming relationships for its Freedom Trainer™. This strategic collaboration would combine SNC’s clean-sheet design with the expertise of Northrop Grumman and GA-ASI, to deliver a zero-compromise training solution for the U.S. Navy’s Undergraduate Jet Training System (UJTS) program.
